HOD Message

Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering at ICEEM. Our department is committed to providing high-quality education in computing disciplines with a focus on academic excellence, innovation, and industry collaboration. We strive to shape students into professionals with deep technical knowledge, strong moral values, and leadership skills to compete at a global level.

Department Vision

To produce globally competent and socially responsible computer engineers capable of contributing to the advancement of society through innovation and technical excellence.

Department Mission

  • Provide quality education with strong academic foundations.
  • Promote research, development, and technical innovation.
  • Establish strong ties with industry for practical exposure.
  • Instill professional ethics and lifelong learning skills in students.
