About ICEEM College

International Centre of Excellence in Engineering and Management (ICEEM)

Vision

To be a leading Educational Institute in the field of Technology, Management, and Research to serve the global needs of Academia, Industry, and Society.

Mission

  • Impart Education for Academic Excellence in the field of Technology and Management.
  • Inculcate high moral, ethical, and professional standards among our students for their holistic development.
  • Provide modern infrastructure and Techno-Managerial learning environment for innovation and research.

Affiliations & Accreditations

ICEEM is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, recognized by DTE (Govt. of Maharashtra), and affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Chh. Sambhajinagar.

Our Journey So Far

ICEEM was established in 2011 by Shri. Bhujangrao Kulkarni (Retd. IAS), with the vision to promote excellence in technical and management education. Over the years, the college has grown to include multiple departments and thousands of successful alumni, nurturing engineers and professionals for a better tomorrow.
