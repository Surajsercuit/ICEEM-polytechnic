About College

ICEEM – International Centre of Excellence in Engineering and Management, Chh. Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), was established in 2011 under the leadership of our Founder-Chairman, Shri. Bhujangrao Kulkarni (Retd. IAS). The institute aims at providing Quality Education in Engineering & Management, nurturing future Engineers, Entrepreneurs, and Managers.

ICEEM is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, recognized by DTE (Govt. of Maharashtra), and affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Chh. Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad). The institute has made steady progress over the last twelve years towards its vision of becoming one of the top hundred "Technical Campuses" in India by 2025.
